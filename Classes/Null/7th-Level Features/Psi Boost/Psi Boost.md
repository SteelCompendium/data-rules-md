# Psi Boost

Whenever you use a psionic action or maneuver, you can spend additional clarity to apply a Psi Boost to it and enhance its effects. You can apply multiple Psi Boosts to an action, but only 1 instance of each Psi Boost. You have access to the following Psi Boosts:

[SHARPENED POWER (1 DISCIPLINE)](./Sharpened%20Power.md)

[HEIGHTENED POWER (1 DISCIPLINE)](./Heightened%20Power.md)

[EXTENDED POWER (1 DISCIPLINE)](./Extended%20Power.md)

[DYNAMIC POWER (1 DISCIPLINE)](./Dynamic%20Power.md)

[EXPANDED POWER (3 DISCIPLINE)](./Expanded%20Power.md)

[SHARED POWER (5 DISCIPLINE)](./Shared%20Power.md)

[MAGNIFIED POWER (5 DISCIPLINE)](./Magnified%20Power.md)
