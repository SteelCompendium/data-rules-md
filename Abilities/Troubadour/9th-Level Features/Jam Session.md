###### Jam Session (11 Drama)

*Your jam session creates new genres that compel everyone to get up and move.*

| --------------- | ----------------------------: |
| **📏 5 burst**  | **🎯 Each enemy in the area** |

**Power Roll + Presence:**

- **≤11:** 8 sonic damage
- **12-16:** 11 sonic damage
- **17+:** 15 sonic damage
